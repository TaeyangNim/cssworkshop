Ingredients

1 Package of Pasta 1 Bottle of Tomato Pasta Sauce
10 Cloves Garlic 5 White Mushrooms
1 Tablespoon Olive Oil 10 Cherry Tomatoes
Parmesan Chesse 110g Bacon
2 Sausages Half Onion
Salt Parsley powder
Broccoli Pepper


Preperation

Chop all the preperations on one tray.
Cut the stem of the broccoli to make it into small pieces, and boil them slightly in a salted water
Boil the Water and put half teaspoon of salt and 1 teaspoon of olive oil.
Put the noodle into the boiling water, and boil it for 8 minuites



Put 1 spoon of olive oil, and pan fry the onion
When onion cook, put bacon and ham at the same time
When bacon start to cook, put tomato pasta sauce, tomato, white mushrooms, and broccoli.
Put the cooked pasta noodle, salt, and pepper.
When everything cooked, put it on the plate, and put parsley powdel and parmesan chesse on top of it.



About

Cooking time: 30 minuites

Portion: Two People
